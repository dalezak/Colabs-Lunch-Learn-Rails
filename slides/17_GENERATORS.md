```
rails generate controller Pages index --no-javascripts --no-stylesheets --no-assets --no-fixture
```

- `app/views/pages/index.html.erb`
- `app/helpers/pages_helper.rb`
- `test/controllers/pages_controller_test.rb`

rails s
